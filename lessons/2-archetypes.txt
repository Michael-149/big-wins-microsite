<p><strong>Examples:</strong> Carol Dweck (Growth Mindset), William Nordhaus (DICE Model), Michael Kremer (Advance Market Commitments)</p>
<p>Individual researchers champion a coherent idea over decades, iterating through theory, empirical validation, and institutional implementation. <p><strong>Key characteristics:</strong></p>
<ul>

<li><strong>Patient capital:</strong> Require long-term flexible funding to pivot and iterate as understanding deepens</li>
</ul>
<p>Carol Dweck spent 40 years developing growth mindset from academic theory to classroom practice. William Nordhaus refined DICE for 32 years before it became the federal government's primary climate-economy tool. These weren't quick winsâ€”they were sustained campaigns.</p>
